## Corrections Required

1. **Khomeini "drinking poison"** — Actual quote is "worse than drinking poison" or "drinking a chalice of poison." Should be presented as paraphrase, not direct quote.
2. **"One million Iranians killed or wounded"** — The ~1 million figure is typically the combined total for both sides. Iranian casualties alone are usually estimated at 500,000-1,000,000. Wording should be softened to "hundreds of thousands" or qualified.
3. **Transjordan as "consolation prize" for Hashemites "expelled from the Hejaz"** — Transjordan was created (1921) BEFORE the Hashemites lost the Hejaz (1925). The consolation framing is anachronistic.
4. **Shah restored to "absolute power"** — Consolidation of absolute power was gradual, not immediate upon restoration.
5. **Hafez quote** — Cannot be verified to any published Hafez translation. Likely a composite or fabricated rendering. Should be removed or replaced with a verifiable translation.
6. **PLO expulsion → Hamas/PIJ** — Oversimplified causal chain. PIJ predates PLO's departure; Hamas emerged from the First Intifada context more than from the PLO vacuum.

## Final Assessment

The essay is **substantially accurate** in its historical claims. Out of 54+ discrete factual claims checked, **zero are outright false**. Six are partially correct with minor inaccuracies. The Hafez quote is the most concerning item — it cannot be sourced and may be fabricated. All 2026 conflict details align with the briefing state file. The essay demonstrates strong command of Middle Eastern history with only minor imprecisions typical of narrative academic writing.
