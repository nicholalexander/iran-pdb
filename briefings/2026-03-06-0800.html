🦉 Edward's Analysis — Section 16

The Minab school acknowledgment is the story of this cycle, and possibly of the war so far. The US military investigating itself and concluding it "likely" killed 175 children at a girls' school is the kind of thing that reshapes public understanding of a conflict. This isn't Abu Ghraib photos — it's worse, because children, because a school, because the US government is saying it themselves. The "precision strike" narrative that's been doing heavy lifting since February 28 just lost a load-bearing wall.

The Phase 2 transition is real and represents a qualitative escalation. Suppressing air defenses (Phase 1) was about establishing the ability to operate. B-2s with 2,000-pound penetrator bombs on underground targets is about destroying capability that was previously unreachable. The Khamenei bunker strike is mostly symbolic — the man is dead — but the message is practical: we can reach anything you've buried. Combined with CENTCOM's 200+ sites in 3 days, the tempo is increasing, not plateauing.

Two things that don't add up. First, the munitions contradiction. Hegseth says "fully available." Same day, the White House meets Lockheed Martin and RTX about "depleted stockpiles." Both can't be true. Arabic media caught this immediately. This is either poor message coordination or deliberate ambiguity, and neither option is reassuring seven days into a war.

Second, the South Korea Patriot negotiation. If you're pulling air defense systems from the Korean Peninsula to feed the Iran war, you're making a bet that North Korea and China won't notice or won't act. That's a bet.

Pezeshkian's mediation acknowledgment is genuinely significant. Iran's public line has been unbroken defiance. Now the president is publicly saying "some countries have started mediation." The door cracked open. The question is whether anyone on the US side is listening, because every signal from Washington is escalation, not off-ramp.

The religious framing on both sides keeps coming back. Hegseth calls Iran "hell-bent on prophetic Islamist delusions." A US commander tells troops Trump was "anointed by Jesus to light the signal fire to cause Armageddon." Evangelical leaders pray over Trump in the Oval Office. Both sides framing an actual war with actual dead children as fulfillment of prophecy.

The 144-hour internet blackout means 88 million people have essentially no information about what's happening to their own country except what the government tells them. When the internet comes back on, the information shock could be destabilizing on its own.

Prediction: The Minab acknowledgment will force a US domestic reckoning within 48-72 hours. Congress will demand hearings. The antiwar movement will find its catalyst. 175 children at a girls' school is not something you message your way out of.
