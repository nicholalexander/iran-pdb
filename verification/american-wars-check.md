## Errors Requiring Correction

### ❌ Critical Errors

1. **McNaughton memo misattributed to McNamara (Claim #13):** The 70%/20%/10% memo was written by **John T. McNaughton** (Assistant Secretary of Defense) to McNamara, not by McNamara to Johnson. This is a significant misattribution.

2. **Rolling Thunder tonnage: 643,000 tons (Claim #73):** The actual figure is **864,000 tons** (DoD, Navy Historical Center). The 643,000 figure may be a confusion with the Korean War's 653,000 tons.

3. **Vietnam coastline "17,000 miles" (Claim #15):** Vietnam's coastline is approximately **3,260 km (~2,025 miles)**. 17,000 miles is off by nearly an order of magnitude. This may be a confused figure.

### ⚠️ Notable Inaccuracies

4. **Troop peak "536,100" in "1968" (Claim #11):** Peak was in **1969**, not 1968. Figure is approximately correct but commonly cited as 536,000 or 543,482.

5. **Bombs "more than twice" WWII (Claim #71):** Actually closer to **triple** (~7.66M tons vs ~2.7M for WWII).

6. **Draft inductions "300,000 per year" (Claim #52):** 1969 actual was **283,586**.

7. **Iraq deaths "4,431" (Claim #28):** This is the OIF-only figure; total Iraq including OND is ~4,486-4,497.

8. **Afghanistan deaths "2,461" (Conclusion):** Standard DoD figure is **2,402**; iCasualties lists 2,455. The essay's number is slightly high.
