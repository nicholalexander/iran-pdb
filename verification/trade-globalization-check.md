## Corrections Required

1. **Royal Road courier time: "seven days" → "nine days"** (Herodotus, Wikipedia, Britannica all say nine)
2. **Carter Doctrine duration: "forty-seven years" → "forty-six years"** (1980 to 2026)
3. **Keynes mislabeled as "historian"** — should be "economist" (or "economist John Maynard Keynes, writing in 1919")
4. **"Seventy-two hours into Operation Epic Fury" on March 1** — War began Feb 28; March 1 morning is ~18-24 hours in, not 72
5. **Iran 1979 production drop** — "less than one million" overstates the collapse; initial drop was to ~1.5M bpd
6. **Oscar Wilde quote** — original refers to "cynic," not "economist"; essay's paraphrase is acknowledged with "reportedly" but readers may take it as literal
